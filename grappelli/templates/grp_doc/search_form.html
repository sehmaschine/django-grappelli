Search-Form

Basics

The search-form is meant to appear in a .grp-row within a .grp-module.

Basically it is a form.grp-search-form containing an input.grp-searchfield and a button.grp-search-button.

Make sure to wrap the search-form with spaceless-tags to avoid whitespaces.
